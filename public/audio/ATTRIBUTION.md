### itch.io sci-fi theme packs (for future swaps or extra layers)

itch.io assets usually download as a zip from the creator page (no stable hotlink). Good **sci-fi / space** packs to browse:

- [Free Sci-Fi Game Music Pack — alkakrab](https://alkakrab.itch.io/free-sci-fi-game-music-pack) — commercial OK; license PDF in zip; credit “Music by alkakrab”.
- [Ambient Space — marc_davs](https://marc-davs.itch.io/ambient-space) — attribution optional; WAV recommended for seamless loops.
- [Space Ambient Theme — Theodore Kerr](https://theodore-kerr.itch.io/sci-fi-space-ambient-theme) — **CC BY 4.0** (attribution required if used).
- [Space Ambient Music Free! — Boomism Beats](https://boomism-beats.itch.io/space-ambient-music-free) — check license terms in download.
